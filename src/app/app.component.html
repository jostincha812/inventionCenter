Over 50 Years of Experience in Event Management

Atlanta, a unique destination in the heart of the South, is also host to one of the most convenient and connected convention spaces. The Atlanta Convention Center at AmericasMart is the city’s best-value meetings, special events and tradeshow facility. Our team of experts has more than 50 years of experience in operating, managing, and providing solutions for events of any size. Come experience what being connected can do for you. Contact us today!
